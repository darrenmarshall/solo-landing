Using Solo

Where do I get backing tracks?
Any audio file works — MP3, M4A, WAV, AIFF, and more. Download karaoke or instrumental tracks from music stores, YouTube, or anywhere online. Once you have the file, import it from the Files app when setting up a session.
Where do I get lyrics?
Solo can fetch time-synced lyrics automatically — enter the song title and artist in Setup and tap Fetch. You can also paste any plain-text lyrics directly into the lyrics field. When LRC timed lyrics are found, timestamps will appear in the field so you know sync is active.
Does Solo work offline?
Yes. Import, record, review, and export all work without an internet connection. Spotify search and automatic lyrics fetching are optional enhancements — the app works fully without them.
Do I need an account?
No. Solo has no login, no social feed, and no cloud account. Everything stays on your device. You don't need to create an account to use any part of the app.
Where are my recordings stored?
Recordings are saved locally on your iPhone in the app's private Documents folder. Export any take from the Catalog to save it elsewhere or share it. Free users have access to their most recent recording; Pro users have unlimited history.
Can I export my recordings?
Yes — tap any recording in the Catalog and use the export icon to share or save the file. Free users can export as M4A (AAC). Pro users can also export as WAV, AIFF, or Apple Lossless (ALAC). Bulk export is on the roadmap.
What is layer recording?
Layer recording (Pro) lets you record a second vocal take on top of your first without leaving the session. The original recording plays back while you sing over it, and both tracks are mixed together in the final file.
What is Preset Studio?
Preset Studio (Pro) is a full 6-node vocal processing chain — parametric EQ, dynamics processor, reverb, distortion, pitch correction, and spatial audio. You can dial in your sound and save it as a named preset to reuse across sessions.
How do I cancel my Solo Pro subscription?
Open the Settings app on your iPhone → tap your name → Subscriptions → Solo → Cancel Subscription. Your Pro access continues until the end of the current billing period.

Privacy

Does Solo collect any personal data?
No. Solo does not collect, transmit, or store any personal data on external servers. There are no user accounts, no analytics SDKs, and no advertising networks. Everything you create stays on your device unless you explicitly export or share it.
Why does Solo need microphone access?
Solo needs microphone access to record your voice during practice sessions. Audio is captured only while you are actively recording within the app. Recordings are saved locally to your device and are never transmitted anywhere.
Why does Solo need photo library access?
Solo asks for photo library access only if you choose to set a custom cover image for a recording. Photos are accessed solely for this purpose and are not stored externally or transmitted.
Does Solo make any network requests?
Solo may optionally connect to Spotify (song metadata search), Musixmatch, LRCLIB, and Genius (lyrics lookup) if those features are configured. These calls send only the song title and artist you type — no personal information. All network features are optional and the app works fully without them.
Will my audio ever be uploaded?
No. Vocal processing is designed to run entirely on-device. Solo does not upload your recordings to any server. If we ever add network-based features, they will be clearly labeled and strictly opt-in.
How does Solo handle payments?
All payments are handled entirely by Apple through the App Store. Solo never receives, processes, or stores any payment or billing information. For details on how Apple handles purchase data, refer to Apple's Privacy Policy.
